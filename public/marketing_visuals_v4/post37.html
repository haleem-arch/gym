Deadlift Safety Cues

Deadlift Cues
Maintain safe mechanics during compound pulling routines.
Deadlift Form Rules
• Keep bar in contact with shins during pull.
• Engage lats and brace core before lifting weight.
• Hinge hips back; do not round lower back.
* Restrict sets if lower back fatigue is reported
Spine Defense
Reminds clients to keep straight postures under heavy loads.
